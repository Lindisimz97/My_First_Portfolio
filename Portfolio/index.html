Hey there! I’m Simlindile Stallenberg, a front-end developer with a knack for turning tech challenges into interactive triumphs. By day, I’m all about crafting sleek web experiences, but by night, I’m a puzzle enthusiast, board game aficionado, and investigative documentary junkie.

As a night owl with a flair for problem-solving, I find myself constantly pondering new ideas and exploring the mysteries of the world.
